Login Support

1. Quick check – can you sign in on the official site?

Open the official SchoolSoft page: schoolsoft.se.

  • Username: [first_name].[last_name] (example: donald.trump)
  • Password: try the same one you use for your Google account – they’re often identical.

Still stuck? Jump to step 3 – reset your password.

2. Works on SchoolSoft, but not on SchoolSoft+?

Go to SchoolSoft + and enter the exact same credentials you just used on the official site.

3. Can’t sign in anywhere? Reset your password

  1. Visit schoolsoft.se and click Need help logging in?
  2. Fill in:
    • Username: [first_name].[last_name]
    • E-mail: your school e-mail address
  3. You’ll receive an e-mail with a temporary password.
  4. Sign in with that temporary password (or use external login – either works).
  5. Go to Profile ▸ Settings, scroll to Change password, enter the temporary password once and your new password twice, then save.

Your new password now works on both the official SchoolSoft and SchoolSoft+.
